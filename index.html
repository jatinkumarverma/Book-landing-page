Overview

Harsh was a typical schoolboy, lost in his own world of books, until the day he met Vartika in class fourth. As soon as he laid his eyes on her, he knew he was in love. She was the most beautiful girl he had ever seen, with her sparkling eyes and a smile that could light up the room.

Harsh was too shy to approach her. He would steal glances at her, hoping that one day, he would gather the courage to talk to her. But fate had different plans. After their first meeting, Harsh never got courage to spoke her. They went their separate ways, lost in the endless sea, with only a faint memory.

Years passed, and Harsh thought he had moved on. But when Vartika reappeared in his life during graduation, all those feelings came rushing back. This time, however, he was determined to make a move and not let her slip away again.

Will Harsh finally confess his feelings to Vartika and win her heart? Or will he end up with rejection? Follow the journey of Harsh and Vartika's love story, full of laughter, tears, and unexpected twists and turns, in this romantic comedy novel.

A Note From the Author

Love is not something which you can receive by an email whenever you need it; it is tough finding love. Anyway, as we go finding true love, we all experience turbulence, speed bumps, ugly turns, tears, tonnes of ice cream, assholes, bitches … but do we stop? We do not. We fall in love, hoping that things will turn out just fine, and more often than not, they do not. However, if they do, it makes for a great love story. What if it does or does not? This book is about when it almost doesn’t. And some other unrelated things.
